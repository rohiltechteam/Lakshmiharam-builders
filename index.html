How It Works

1. Plan Approval

We handle all legal documentation and approvals from local authorities, ensuring your construction complies with government regulations.

Detailed 2D architectural drawings are prepared to clearly represent room layouts, dimensions, and structural design.

3. 3D Plan

A realistic 3D floor plan helps you visualize your home design before construction begins.

4. 3D Elevation

High-quality 3D elevation gives you a clear view of your building’s exterior look, including colors, textures, and architectural style.

Share your vision with our experienced architects. Using advanced technology and expert knowledge, we deliver tailored solutions that align with your budget and requirements.

Secure your project with a nominal booking fee of just 0.5% of the total cost. Enjoy fixed pricing and access to your project dashboard with real-time updates.
